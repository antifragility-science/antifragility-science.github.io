<div class="team-intro">
  We are a team of scientists coming from different domains. What connects us is a shared
  language of dynamical systems: we study how information, matter, and energy flow through
  bounded complex systems. The strength of this interdisciplinary group is that we use a
  common mathematical and conceptual toolbox, while working at different scales and in
  very different application areas.
